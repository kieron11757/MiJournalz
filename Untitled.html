‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How do I backup my journal entries?
MiJournalz automatically syncs your entries to iCloud. To ensure backup is enabled, go to Settings > iCloud and make sure MiJournalz is turned on. You can also export your entries as PDF or text files from the app's export feature.
Can I use the app offline?
Yes! MiJournalz works completely offline. You can write, edit, and organize your journal entries without an internet connection. Your entries will sync to iCloud when you're back online.
How do I restore my data if I reinstall the app?
If you have iCloud sync enabled, simply sign in with the same Apple ID when reinstalling the app. Your data will automatically restore. If you don't have iCloud sync, you can restore from a backup using the import feature in Settings.
Is my journal data private and secure?
Absolutely! Your journal entries are encrypted and stored securely. We use end-to-end encryption for iCloud sync, and your data is never shared with third parties. Your privacy is our top priority.
How do I change the app's theme or appearance?
Go to Settings > Appearance to customize your journaling experience. You can choose from different themes, font sizes, and color schemes to make the app feel personal to you.
